3.3    CAPACITY.

Cargo hold and tank capacities ( 100% ) shall be as follows. 

Cargo Holds  (Grain) abt. 77,500  m3.

(Bale) abt. 73,500  m3.

Fuel oil tanks abt. 2,300  m3.

Diesel oil tanks abt. 185  m3.

Fresh water tank abt. 330  m3.

Drinking water tank abt. 330  m3.

Water ballast tanks ( incl. No.3 cargo hold ) abt.  34,050  m3 .

Sewage holding tank abt. 45  m3.
