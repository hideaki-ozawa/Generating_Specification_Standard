2.2    RULE AND REGULATION.
  
The Vessel shall be registered under the flag of PANAMA.  

The following rules and regulations effective at the date of signing the Shipbuilding Contract shall be applied.
  
(1)  Maritime Regulation of the Registered Country. 

(2)  Rules and Regulations for the Construction and Classification of Ships 2015.  

(3)  International Convention on Load Lines, 1966 and its PROTOCOL 1988  ( incl. 2003, 2004, 2005, 2006, 2008, 2012 and 2013 Amendments ).  

(4)  International Convention for the Safety of Life at Sea, 1974, its PROTOCOL 1978 and its PROTOCOL 1988 ( incl. 1981, 1983, 1988, 1989, 1990, 1991, 1992, 1994, 1995, 1996, 1997, 1998,   1999, 2000, 2001, 2002, 2003, 2004, 2005, 2006, 2007, 2008 2009, 2010, 2011,   2012 and 2013 Amendments, excluding Chapter II-1 Reg.3-10 Goal-Based Ship   Construction Standards for Bulk Carriers and Oil Tankers of 2010 Amendments). 
          
(5)  International Convention for the Prevention of Pollution from Ships, 1973 its PROTOCOL of 1978 and 1997 (Annex I, IV, V and VI)   (  incl. 1984, 1985, 1987, 1989, 1990, 1991, 1992, 1994, 1995, 1996, 1997, 1999, 2000, 2001, 2003, 2004, 2005, 2006, 2007, 2008, 2009, 2010, 2011, 2012 and 2013 Amendments and Annex VI Reg.13-Tier II restriction of 2008 Amendment). 

(6)  International Regulations for Preventing Collision at Sea, 1972   ( incl. 1981, 1987, 1989, 1993, 2001 and 2007 Amendments). 

(7)  Radio Regulations of International Telecommunication Union, Edition of 2012. 

(8)  International Convention on Tonnage Measurement of Ships, 1969.  

(9)  International Convention on the Control of Harmful Anti-fouling Systems on Ships, 2001. 

(10) International Convention for the Control and Management of Ships’ Ballast Water and Sediments, 2004. 

(11)  International Code for the Safe Carriage of Grain in Bulk (International Grain Code)(IMO Resolution MSC. 23(59)).
     
(12)  Rules of Navigation of the Suez Canal Authority, 1995 ( incl. Tonnage Measurement Rules ).    

(13)  Rules and Regulations Governing Navigation of Panama Canal and Adjacent Waters(as “Panamax Plus” for OP NOTICE TO SHIPPING of PANAMA CANAL AUTHORITY ) and Rules for the Measurement of Vessels shall be applied as far as practicable.
 
(14)  Pollution Prevention Regulation of USCG for foreign flag Vessel without certificate.
