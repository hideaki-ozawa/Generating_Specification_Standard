5.6    DISPENSATION.  
For the second and successive sister Vessels ( incl. other owner’s Vessel ), the following test may be dispensed with subject to approval of the Classification Society and other regulatory bodies.  
(1) Progressive speed test at normal output of main engine (one (1) double run).   
(2) Progressive speed test at 50% load of maximum output of main engine.   
(3) Turning test.   
(4) Zig-zag test.   
(5) Torsional vibration measurement for propeller shafting.   
(6) The test for the purpose of making the “MANEUVERING INFORMATION”required by the US rule.   
(7) Inclining test.
