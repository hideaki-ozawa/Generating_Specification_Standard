5.6    DISPENSATION.

For the second and successive sister Vessels ( incl. other owner’s Vessel ), the following test may be dispensed with subject to approval of the Classification Society and other regulatory bodies. 

The result of light weight measurement and deadweight calculation included inclining test result of the sister Vessel shall be submitted to the Owner. 

(1) Progressive speed test on the Builder’s favourable draught and trim condition at 1/2 load of maximum output of main engine. 

(2) Turning test. 

(3) Zig-zag test. 

(4) Torsional vibration measurement for propeller shafting. 

(5) The test for the purpose of making the “MANEUVERING INFORMATION”   required by the US rule.

(6) Inclining test.
