Shaft seal of screw pumps shall be provided with mechanical seal.

Screw pumps shall develop rated capacity and discharge pressure when operating with the 
under-said suction conditions.

The materials of main parts of the screw pumps shall be as follows :.

 Casing Cast iron 
 Casing cover Cast iron 
 Power rotor Carbon steel 
 Idle rotor Ductile steel.
